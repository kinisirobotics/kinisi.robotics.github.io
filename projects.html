Quadrotors for Agriculture

Quadrotors are extremely efficient than humans when it comes to inspection of vast areas and detection of anomalies. More specifically, these quadrotors are trustful in the applications such as agriculture and defense. In agricultural applications, quadrotors and its counterparts can be used for spraying fertilizers and pesticides on crops and detecting pests using machine learning algorithms. An average human takes about 2 hours to spray fertilizer on half an acre of land; a quadrotor equipped with necessary appendages can accomplish the same task in less than 15 minutes when operated with a radio control. The same quadrotor with path planning algorithms embedded in it would make the process even more efficient and human indepedent. After all it is much better to use machines such as quadrotors because humans are made for cerebral tasks.
